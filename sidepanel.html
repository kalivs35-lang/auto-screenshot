Auto Screenshot

Instructions

  1. Set the capture Interval below.
  2. Click "Select Tab & Start Capturing".
  3. Choose the screen or tab to capture in the popup.
  4. Screenshots will auto-download to your Downloads folder.
  5. If prompted by your browser, click "Allow" to permit downloading multiple files automatically.
💡 Important Tip - Prevent Save Prompts:
To stop your browser from asking where to save every single screenshot, turn OFF "Ask where to save each file before downloading" in your browser settings.
  • Chrome: chrome://settings/downloads
  • Brave: brave://settings/downloads
  • Edge: edge://settings/downloads
  • Opera: opera://settings/downloads
  • Vivaldi: vivaldi://settings/downloads
